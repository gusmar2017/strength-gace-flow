# Strength, Grace and Flow - User Persona

## Who We Serve

### Demographics
- **Age Range**: 22-45 years old
- **Gender**: Women
- **Life Stages**:
  - Some haven't yet thought about pregnancy
  - Some are actively trying to conceive
  - Some have already had children
  - All are navigating different phases of their hormonal journey

### Psychographics
- Seeking a **tranquil space** to achieve physiological goals
- Want to understand their bodies and hormonal patterns
- Value gentle, non-judgmental support
- Desire balance and harmony in their wellness journey
- Looking for wisdom and insight, not just data
- Appreciate mindfulness and intentionality

## What They Need From Us

### A Calm Sanctuary
This app should feel like a peaceful moment in their day - a place to check in with themselves, free from pressure, judgment, or clinical coldness.

### Respectful Language
Language that honors where they are in their journey, whether they're tracking cycles for awareness, fertility, or simply understanding their body's natural rhythm.

### Empowerment Through Clarity
Minimal, reflective guidance that helps them understand patterns without overwhelming them with information or making them feel like they need to be "fixed."

### Universal Inclusivity
Terminology that works for everyone - whether they're 25 and learning about their cycle for the first time, 35 and trying to conceive, or 42 and navigating hormonal changes.

## How They Want to Feel

- **Supported** - like they have a wise friend who understands
- **Peaceful** - this is a calm, gentle space
- **Empowered** - they're learning about their own body
- **Seen** - their unique journey is valid and honored
- **Balanced** - in harmony with their natural rhythms

## What They Don't Want

- Clinical, medical language that feels cold
- Overly cutesy or diminutive language that infantilizes
- Assumptions about their reproductive goals
- Pressure or judgment about their choices
- Over-explanation or health anxiety
- Language that feels rushed or transactional
