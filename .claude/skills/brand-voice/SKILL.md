## Brand Voice Overview: Strength, Grace, Flow

### Core Pillars
1. **Strength**: Empowering, confident, supportive
2. **Grace**: Gentle, elegant, respectful
3. **Flow**: Natural, rhythmic, harmonious

## Tone Characteristics

### What We Are
- Gentle: soft, warm, caring
- Reflective: thoughtful, mindful, present
- Natural: organic language, not clinical
- Wise: knowledgeable without being preachy
- Minimal: clear and concise, not over-explaining
- Neutral: inclusive of all life stages and goals

### What We're Not
- Clinical or medical-sounding
- Overly cheerful or enthusiastic
- Cutesy or using slang
- Assumptive about reproductive goals
- Anxious or fear-based
- Rushed or transactional

## Language Framework

### Natural Language Preferences
- **Preferred Terms**:
  - period
  - cycle
  - flow
  - rhythm
  - energy levels
  - body's rhythm

- **Avoid**:
  - menstruation (too clinical)
  - "that time of month" (too coy)
  - Assumptive language about fertility or reproduction

### Core Vocabulary
Words that align with our brand and feel right:
- balance
- harmony
- rhythm
- alignment
- flow
- cycle
- energy
- patterns
- awareness
- wisdom

## Universal & Inclusive Language

### Principles
- Use "Your cycle" instead of specific reproductive terms
- Focus on universal experiences
- Neutral, empowering language for all life stages

### Examples
- ✅ "Your body's rhythm"
- ✅ "Day 1 of your cycle"
- ✅ "Notice any shifts in your energy?"
- ❌ "You're in your fertile window"
- ❌ "Time to conceive!"

## Writing Style Guide

### Sentence Structure
- Short and clear
- Present and grounded
- Active voice (you, your, this)
- Minimal punctuation
- Prefer periods over exclamation points

### Tone Variations
1. **Gentle Questions**:
   - Check-ins and reflections
   - "How are you feeling today?"
   - "Notice any shifts in your energy?"

2. **Calm Statements**:
   - Information and guidance
   - "You're in your follicular phase"
   - "Your energy may feel more expansive today"

3. **Invitations**:
   - Encourage action softly
   - "Take a moment to log how you're feeling"
   - "Reflect on your energy today"

## Design System Integration

### Color Palette
- Primary colors for emphasis and interaction
  - Soft Sand (`#F6F1EA`): Background
  - Deep Charcoal (`#2E2E2E`): Primary text
  - Deep Terracotta (`#B35C44`): Accents, active states
  - Dusty Blue (`#6F8F9B`): Secondary accents

### Typography
- **Headlines**: Playfair Display
  - Elegant, feminine serif
  - Weights: Regular (400), Medium (500), Bold (700)

- **Body Text**: Inter
  - Clean, highly readable sans-serif
  - Weights: Regular (400), Medium (500), SemiBold (600)

### Phase-Specific Design
Each menstrual phase has a designated color:
- Menstrual: Deep Terracotta
- Follicular: Dusty Blue
- Ovulatory: Soft Clay Neutral
- Luteal: Blue-Clay Blend

## Copywriting Standards

### Context-Specific Guidelines

#### Onboarding & Education
- Minimal, grounded explanations
- Focus on awareness over optimization
- Example: "Your cycle has four phases, each with its own energy"

#### Daily Check-ins
- Gentle, present-moment language
- "How's your energy today?"
- "Notice any changes?"

#### Data & Insights
- Reflective, observational
- "You tend to feel more energized during this phase"
- Avoid: "Studies show you should feel X"

#### Errors & Empty States
- Supportive, never scolding
- "When you're ready, log your first entry"
- Avoid: "No data yet - start tracking!"

#### Notifications
- Soft reminders, never demanding
- "Time for your evening reflection"
- "Your cycle may begin in the next few days"
